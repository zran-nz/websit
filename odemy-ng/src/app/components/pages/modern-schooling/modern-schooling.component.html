Competitive advantages

Competitive Advantages

Global community: Removes the distance between different educators around the world to collaborate and share unique teaching strategies and resources through a streamlined, user-friendly online platform.

Unit/Lesson Planning function: Access innovative and exceptional teaching resources that are efficiently and logically organised

Visible thinking tools: Use tools that will enable students to explore, clarify, and communicate their thinking processes and ideas within the classroom.

Differentiated instruction tool: Using different approaches and resources, deliver classroom content that is easily adapted for individual students’ needs.
